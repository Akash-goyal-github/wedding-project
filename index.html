Anant Mahal

Patrakar Colony, Near Raheja Tower, Mansarovar, Jaipur

Anant Mahal is one of Jaipur’s finest royal banquet palaces — a regal haven with ornate arches, marble corridors, and lush garden terraces set in the heart of Mansarovar, Jaipur.

Our celebration will fill every golden hallway and flower-strewn garden with music, laughter, and the timeless fragrance of marigold.
